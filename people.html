Meet the Team

Tiffany Amariuta, Ph.D.

Principal Investigator


Tiffany started her group at UCSD in July of 2022. For more information about her and the research focus of the group, please see the home, about, and publications pages.

Michelle Franc Ragsac, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral Scholar

Michelle is a Postdoctoral Scholar with a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics & Systems Biology, specializing in genomic regulation during embryonic development. She is passionate about exploring the genetic foundations of developmental and childhood diseases. In her free time, she enjoys discovering new restaurants in San Diego and spending time with her Shiba Inu, Yuuki.

Ziqi Xu

Bioinformatics & Systems Biology Ph.D. Student

Ziqi is currently a second-year PhD student in the Bioinformatics and Systems Biology (BISB) program. Her research interest lies in developing computational and statistical methods to understand genetic contributions to diseases. Her current project focuses on estimating heritability from single cell gene expression data and investigating co-regulation across genes and cell types using GWAS and single-cell RNA-seq data. Ziqi enjoys spending her spare time in dog parks and zoos.

Ishaan Gupta

Computer Science & Engineering Ph.D. Student

Ishaan is a PhD student in the Computer Science department and is driven to improve our ability to predict immune responses and disorders in patients. He is currently using gene expression prediction models to find causal cell types and states in autoimmune disorders and immune cell mechanisms. In his free time, Ishaan loves to try food and recipes from across the globe or go hiking.

Xiaoyu Gui

Undergraduate Researcher

Xiaoyu is a third-year undergraduate majoring in Data Science with two minors in General Biology and Mathematics. Her interest lies in leveraging data science techniques to help minimize the gap in health-related discoveries among populations with different ancestry. In her free time, she enjoys taking photos around the city.

Stephen Golzari

Undergraduate Researcher

Stephen is a third-year undergraduate researcher majoring in Bioengineering/Bioinformatics. He is currently working on incorporating fine-mapping methods to improve multi-ancestry gene expression prediction models. In his free time, Stephen enjoys composing music and playing chess.

Ieva Sereiva

Undergraduate Researcher

Ieva is a third-year undergraduate student studying bioinformatics at UCSD. She is currently working on the development of a pangenome browser, which is aimied at providing a user-friendly interface for visualising and analyzing pangenomic data. In her free time, Ieva likes to read and explore the city with friends.

Nan Huang

Undergraduate Researcher

Nan is a third-year undergraduate student double majoring in Data Science and Math Econ. She is interested in understanding the genetic basis of childhood asthma, aiming to develop clinical risk models that integrate genetic, transcriptomic, and non-genetic risk factors to enhance early asthma detection and personalized treatment strategies. Outside the lab, Nan enjoys exploring new restaurants, cooking recipes, and different types of music.
